# World State (Midgaard)

## Known rooms and connections

- **Starting room** (pitch black, unnamed): exits north (The Common Square), down (unknown, too dark).
- **The Common Square**: exits n/e/s/w. West = poor alley, east = dark alley, north = Market Square, south = starting room (nasty smell). A beastly fido roams here.
- **Market Square** (Square of Midgaard, statue in center): exits n/e/s/w. North = Temple Square, south = Common Square, east/west = Main Street.
- **Main Street (east of Market Square)**: exits n/e/s/w. North = General Store, south = Pet Shop, east = continues on Main Street, west = Market Square.
- **Main Street (further east)**: exits n/e/s/w. North = Weapon Shop, south = Guild of Swordsmen, east = leaves town, west = back toward Market Square.
- **Main Street (west of Market Square)**: exits n/e/s/w. South = Armory entrance, north = **The Bakery**, east = Market Square.
- **The Bakery**: exit south (back to Main Street). NPCs present: a cityguard and the baker (a Peacekeeper was seen here earlier but roams). A small sign sits on the counter.
